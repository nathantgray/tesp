DSOT_ev_model_metadata contains parameters necessary to characterize a physical ev device.
---------------------------------------------
*sale_probability: % of model sold out of total ev models in from 2016-2019
*range(miles): maximum miles the ev can drive with 100% battery w/o charging
*Level_2 max power (kW): maximum kW possible while charging via level-2 charging
*Miles per kWh: mileage efficiency of a modal
*Level_1 max power (kW): maximum kW possible while charging via level-1 charging
*Level_1_usage: % of ev owners using level 1 charging at home (less expensive but much slower)
